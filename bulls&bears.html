About

Bulls & Bears Trading Academy is leading share market training academy in Kolhapur & Maharashtra founded in 2023. Its head office is located at District place Kolhapur in Maharashtra. The academy provides in depth knowledge on investment, stock & F&O trading as well as algo trading to its students throughout India.

It conducts offline as well as online training courses. It has trained more than 1000 students in just 2 years & made extraordinary achievement of making successful traders in society. It is the only trading academy in Maharashtra which shares its own trading profit-loss statement to build a trust in students.

Vision

To enable professionals, Job holders, Housewives & students to start passive income through trading in share market to achieve them time & financial freedom.

Mission

Developing innovative trading techniques with lower risk, requiring less screen time using advanced automation tools.
